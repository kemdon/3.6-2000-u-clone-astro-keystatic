Beeswax Candle Solution

Beeswax candles are a natural and eco-friendly choice,Known for their subtly sweet honey aroma, they emit a warm, golden glow that creates a cozy ambiance. they burn cleanly,making them ideal for indoor air quality. With a rustic charm and sustainable appeal, beeswax candles are a thoughtful addition to any home, blending beauty with environmental responsibility.

Candle type

Different wax materials can be selected based on market positioning and usage scenarios, including soy wax, paraffin wax, beeswax, and mixed wax solutions. We can achieve a professional balance among environmental attributes, combustion stability, fragrance diffusion ability and cost, to meet the needs of retail, gifts and brand customization.

Customized fragrance design

Offer a rich range of fragrance types, including floral, fruity, woody, fresh and seasonal scents. At the same time, support for unscented candles or the development of exclusive fragrances, helping brands create unique olfactory memories and enhancing product recognition.

Color customization

Supports multiple color customization options, including single color, gradient color and special texture effects. Can be precisely tinted according to the brand VI or Pantone color codes to ensure that the appearance of the candles is highly consistent with the overall packaging style.

Shape And Size

From standard cylindrical candles and cup-shaped candles to creative-shaped candles, all can be customized. The weight and size can be flexibly selected, suitable for various applications such as birthday candles, scented candles, home decorations or promotional gifts.

Custom Packaging

LOGO Customization
